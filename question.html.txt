***** Attributes *****
Common_attributes and common_linking_attributes.
***** Description *****
A question in a qandaentry poses a question or states a problem that is addressed by the following answer(s). The answers are optional (some questions have no answers) and may be repeated (some questions have more than one answer).
**** Processing expectations ****
The questions are frequently introduced with a label, such as “A:”. If a question has a label child element, the content of that label is used as the label for the question. The defaultlabel attribute on the nearest ancestor qandaset of a question can be used to indicate that a processing application should automatically generate a label for the question.
