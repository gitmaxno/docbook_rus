***** Attributes *****
Common_attributes and common_linking_attributes.
***** Description *****
The term in a varlistentry identifies the thing that is described or defined by that entry.
**** Processing expectations ****
Formatted as a displayed block. A term is usually formatted to make it stand out with respect to the text that follows. The best presentation depends on several factors, including the number and length of the terms. The termlength attribute on the containing variablelist may influence the presentation of terms. See variablelist.
