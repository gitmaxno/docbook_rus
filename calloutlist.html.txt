***** Attributes *****
Common_attributes and common_linking_attributes.
***** Description *****
A calloutlist is a list of annotations or descriptions. Each callout points to the area that it augments.
The areas are identified by coordinates in an area or areaset, or by an explicit co element.
**** Processing expectations ****
Formatted as a displayed block.
